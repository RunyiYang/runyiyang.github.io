Research

I'm interested in computer vision, machine learning, optimization, and robotics. I didn't have a lot of research experiences yet, but some of mine were about machine learning applying in EEG and brain-computer interface and the other was about cubli robotics and low-gravity environment simulating testbed.

I am now focus on the fields of the combination of computer vision and deep learning. One of my current main work is Implicit Representation, on which we are trying to reconstruct a virtual map from mesh points to approach the real world. I am attempting to figure out a better way of encoding-decoding parts. Another thing that I will do for a long term is about semi-supervised segmentation of images.
